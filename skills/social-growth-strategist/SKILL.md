## Platform Algorithm Intelligence

### Instagram: Four Separate Algorithms

Instagram runs four separate ranking systems — treating each surface differently:

| Surface | Graph Type | Discovery? | Dominant Signals |
|---------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Home Feed | Social + Interest hybrid | Partial (30% recommended) | Relationship closeness, engagement velocity, recency |
| Stories | Pure social graph | No (followers only) | View history, DM frequency, reply rate |
| Explore | Pure interest graph | Yes (non-followers only) | Content quality, topic relevance, engagement speed |
| Reels | Interest-dominant | Yes (55% views from non-followers) | Watch time, DM sends, completion rate |

**The #1 signal for reaching NEW audiences: DM sends/shares** — weighted 3-5x higher than likes (Adam Mosseri, Jan 2025). A post with modest likes but high DM sends reaches far more new people than a heavily-liked post nobody forwards.

**Signal hierarchy for growth (unconnected reach):**
1. DM sends/shares (3-5x weight vs likes)
2. Watch time (dominant for Reels)
3. Saves (signals "worth revisiting")
4. Comments (contributes to dwell time)
5. Likes per reach (more important for connected reach)
6. Profile visits (strong Explore signal)
7. Follows from post (converts strangers)

**Content distribution tiers**: Every post gets an "audition" — first shown to a small group (200-500 users), evaluated in 1-2 hours. 94% of distribution now comes from AI recommendations. The critical threshold: 60%+ retention past 3 seconds correlates with 5-10x total reach.

**December 2025 Originality Update**: Aggregator/AI-slop accounts saw 60-80% reach drops. Original creators saw 40-60% increases. Instagram introduced an "Originality Score" — use AI to enhance human content, not replace it entirely.

### TikTok: Interest-First with Traffic Pool System

TikTok's FYP is purely interest-graph based — zero followers can go viral.

**Four-stage recommendation:**
1. Seed test (200-500 viewers) → 1-3 hour evaluation
2. Expanded group (2,000-10,000) → needs completion rate >25%, engagement >5%
3. Broad distribution (10,000-50,000+) → shares and saves dominate
4. Viral (hundreds of thousands+) → each wave amplifies ~5-10x

**December 2024 "Earthquake" Update**: TikTok replaced the old "first 3 seconds" metric with a **15-20 second sustained engagement threshold**. The algorithm now tracks whether viewers stay engaged throughout, not just the hook. Mid-content quality became equally important.

**Cross-platform repost penalty**: Content detected as cross-posted gets 40% reach reduction (Jan 2025). Must re-record, not just re-upload.

**TikTok SEO (critical)**: 40% of young users now use TikTok instead of Google for search. Keyword placement priority:
1. On-screen text (first 2-3 seconds) — search ranking +400%
2. Spoken words (first 3 seconds) — TikTok indexes speech, +50-100% relevance
3. Caption (first 150 characters) — front-load keywords
4. Hashtags (3-5) — 1 broad + 2-3 vertical + 1 niche

## Content Format Decision Framework

### When to Use What

| Goal | Best Format | Why |
|------|------------|-----|
| Discovery (new audiences) | Reels/TikTok (primary), Carousels (secondary) | 55%+ Reel views from non-followers; Reels reach rate 37.8% |
| Retention (keep followers) | Stories (primary), Carousels (secondary) | Stories prioritize existing relationships; carousels drive saves |
| Conversion (drive actions) | Stories (links, DMs), Carousels (CTAs) | Direct link stickers + DM triggers |
| Authority building | Carousels (primary) | Highest save rates; "mini-blog" format |
| Community | Stories + Lives | Real-time interaction, polls, Q&As |

### Format Performance Data

**Carousels**: 0.55% average engagement (2x single images), highest save rates. Hootsuite data shows carousels outperform Reels in finance specifically. Sweet spot: 6-10 slides. Hook on slide 1, core value slides 4-8 (one point per slide), CTA on final slide. Exit rate: 23.8% on slide 1, stabilizes to 13.3% by slide 9.

**Reels**: 2.25x more reach than static photos. Optimal length 15-30 seconds for educational content. Hook in first 1.5-3 seconds. Cut every 1-2 seconds to maintain pacing. Always include captions (most viewers watch muted). Instagram-native Reels get 3-5x more reach than reposted TikToks.

**TikTok video length data:**
- 15-30 sec: Highest engagement rate, best completion rate (discovery)
- 1-3 min: Creator Rewards Program minimum (monetization RPM $0.40-$1.00+)
- 3-10 min: Highest average view count (2x that of 6-10 sec videos)

### Recommended Content Mix

**Instagram**: 5-7 Reels/week + 2-3 carousels/week + daily Stories (5-7/day) + max 1 static post/week. Posting 6-9 times/week generates 3.7x baseline growth.

**TikTok**: 2-3 posts/day during cold start (0-1K), then 1-2/day at scale. Mix: 40% short (15-60s) + 25% long (1-3min) + 20% photo carousels + 15% lives.

**Content ratio**: 70% evergreen / 30% trend-reactive. Evergreen content ROI is dramatically higher (search views accumulate over months/years); trend content lifespan is only 3-7 days.

## Hook Formulas (First 3 Seconds)

63% of high-CTR videos hook viewers in the first 3 seconds. Videos with >65% 3-second retention get 4-7x more distribution.

| Formula | Structure | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Bold claim | Counter-intuitive statement (2s) → credibility hint (1s) | Education |
| Curiosity gap | Specific pain point question → visual reinforcement | Tutorials, strategy |
| Result first | Show outcome → then explain how | Transformation, challenges |
| Pattern interrupt | Unexpected visual/sound in frame 1 | Entertainment, brand |
| Direct address | Command-style targeting of specific audience | Community building |
| Controversy | Bold, polarizing opinion | Engagement farming |
| Data-driven | Specific number + outcome promise | Lists, education |

## Bilingual Strategy (English + Traditional Chinese)

Instagram and TikTok don't explicitly penalize multilingual posts, but mixing languages dilutes the algorithm's ability to categorize content and target the right audience. Localized content gets 2.3x higher engagement than single-language global content.

**Recommended approach:**
1. **Start English-primary** — maximum global reach, lingua franca of tech/AI discourse
2. **Integrate Traditional Chinese through**: subtitles on Reels, bilingual carousel slides, Traditional Chinese in first comment (keeps caption clean for SEO), bilingual bio
3. **Use hashtags in BOTH languages** on every post — English for global (#AINews, #FinTech) + Traditional Chinese for Taiwan (#人工智慧, #理財, #投資)
4. **When Taiwan followers exceed 30%** → launch dedicated Traditional Chinese account
5. **Always use Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)**, never Simplified, for Taiwan audiences

For TikTok: separate accounts per language market is strongly recommended. Each account's metadata (captions, hashtags, on-screen text) must be completely in the target language.

## Platform-Specific Content Rules

### Instagram Content Eligibility (for recommendations)
- No watermarks from other platforms
- Original content (not reposts)
- Audio included
- Under 3 minutes
- No more than 10 reposts within 30 days

### TikTok Content Rules
- Cross-platform reposts get 40% reach penalty — re-record, don't re-upload
- AI content must be labeled (no reach penalty for labeled AI content)
- On-screen text in first 2-3 seconds boosts search ranking 400%
- Topic clusters (10+ videos around same keyword) rank 300-500% higher in search

### AI Content Strategy
- AI tools reduce production time by 70%, but "AI Slop" triggers reach penalties
- Use AI to enhance human-created content (ideation, editing, optimization), not as complete replacement
- Instagram December 2025 "Originality Score" detects recycled content
- TikTok detects content from 47 different AI platforms
- Key: AI-assisted with human creative direction and quality control
