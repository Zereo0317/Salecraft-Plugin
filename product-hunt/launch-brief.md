## First Comment (Maker's Story)

Hey PH! I'm the maker of SaleCraft.

Here's what I kept seeing: small product sellers -- handmade soap, local skincare, artisan food -- spending $2,000-5,000/month on marketing agencies that gave them cookie-cutter landing pages and generic "content calendars." Or worse, they'd spend 40 hours learning Canva, copywriting, and Meta ads themselves. Neither option made sense for a one-person brand selling $30 face cream.

So I built SaleCraft -- but I built it differently from every other AI marketing tool.

**The consultation is completely free.** You paste our GitHub repo into any AI you already use -- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Kimi, whatever -- and it becomes your marketing consultant. It asks what you sell, who buys it, what your pain points are. Then it runs a brand audit, competitor scan, funnel analysis, and growth strategy. All free. No account. No credit card. 25 of our 35 skills cost nothing.

We helped a handmade soap seller in Taipei who was spending $800/month on a freelance marketer. She pasted our repo into Claude, got a full brand audit in 10 minutes, discovered her packaging photos were her weakest link (not her pricing like she thought), and ended up generating a 10-page landing page for $67 that outperformed her previous $1,200 agency-built page.

The key insight: **most small sellers don't need more marketing tools. They need a marketing consultant who actually understands their product first.** That's why SaleCraft diagnoses before it builds. The AI won't touch your credit balance until it has a strategy you've approved.

When you do want execution: landing pages ($200/page, ~30 min), reels ($100/sec), social posts ($100/set), Meta ad campaigns -- all generated by AI, all with transparent pricing shown before every charge.

**Launch offer: First 50 users who try the free consultation and share their brand audit screenshot get 500 bonus points ($500 value) when they sign up for a paid account.**

Try the free consultation right now -- just paste https://github.com/connactai/Salecraft-Plugin into any AI chat and say what you sell.

What's the one marketing task you hate doing most? I'd love to hear -- and I'll tell you exactly which of our 35 skills handles it (probably for free).
